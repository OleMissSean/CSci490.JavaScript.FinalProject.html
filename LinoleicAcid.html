Essential Fatty Acid: Linoleic Acid (Omega-6)

Linoleic acid, or omega-6 fatty acid, and linolenic acid, or omega-3 fatty acid, are the two essential fatty acids. They are termed as essential because the body cannot synthesize them and needs to obtain them from the diet.


Good Sources of Linoleic Acid (Omega-6):


• Plant Oils • Nuts • Avocados • Fish • Organic Eggs
