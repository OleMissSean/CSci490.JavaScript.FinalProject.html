Essential Amino Acid: (for infants) Histidine

Histidine regulates the immune defense in the body, allergic reactions and inflammatory processes, so a deficiency of L-histidine can lead to an increased tendency towards infection and the aggravation of symptoms of allergies.


Good Sources of Histidine:


• Grass-Fed Red Meat • Free-Range Poultry • Fish • Dairy • Rice • Wheat • Rye • Seafood • Beans • Organic Eggs • Buckwheat • Corn • Cauliflower • Mushrooms • Potatoes • Bamboo shoots • Bananas • Cantaloupe • Citrus fruits.
