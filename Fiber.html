Essential Vitamin: Fiber

A higher-fiber diet has been shown to lower blood cholesterol levels and prevent constipation. High-fiber foods also tend to contain more nutrients and fewer calories, are digested more slowly, and help us feel full sooner. Adults are recommended 30 to 45 grams a day.


Good Sources of Fiber:


• Brown Rice • Kale • Beans • Avocados • Rasberries • Broccoli • Oats • Green Peas • Cereal
