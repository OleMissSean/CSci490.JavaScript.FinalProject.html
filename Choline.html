Essential Mineral: Choline

Choline is utilized by the body in a variety of ways including aiding nerve signaling, maintenance of cell membranes, transporting triglycerides from the liver, and as a constituent of nervous system tissues in early brain development.


Good Sources of Choline:


• Pastured Eggs • Beef Liver • Grass-Fed Raw Dairy • Nuts and Legumes • Cruciferous Veggies
